About the App

Our revolutionary spatial computing app integrates seamlessly with AR glasses, leveraging advanced technologies to provide real-time environmental awareness, subtitle generation, and translation capabilities. Whether navigating a busy street or engaging in conversations, this app makes the world more accessible and interactive.

Key Features

Environmental Awareness

Detects and highlights critical objects like vehicles and people around you, offering alerts like "Car approaching from the left."

Real-Time Subtitles

Automatically translates speech into on-screen text, enabling seamless communication in multiple languages.

180-Degree Cameras

Comprehensive surround-view cameras enhance your spatial awareness for an immersive experience.

Zoom Feature

Activate zoom with a button or voice command to focus on distant objects with precision.
